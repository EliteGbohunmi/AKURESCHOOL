Admission Requirements

How to Get In

Simple, clear requirements. No confusion just a path forward.

ND (National Diploma)

  • Minimum of 5 O'Level credits including English and Mathematics
  • WAEC, NECO, or NABTEB result accepted
  • JAMB result where applicable
  • Valid credentials submission at point of application
  • Relevant ND certificate in related discipline
  • SIWES / IT completion evidence
  • Academic transcripts from previous institution
  • Minimum of Lower Credit in ND where required
